About Us

At Instacash, we are committed to making finance accessible to individuals, businesses and communities often overlooked by the conventional financial system.

As a registered Non-Deposit Taking Financial Service Provider (NDFSP), our mission is to bridge the gap for the unserved population—those who struggle to access funds through traditional channels or require financial solutions that are faster and more flexible.

Guided by our core values, we strive to break down financial barriers and ensure equal access to financial services for all. We believe that everyone—regardless of their background or circumstances—deserves the opportunity to achieve their financial goals.

With over 15 years of experience in the financial sector, our team of experts understands the unique challenges faced by individuals and small businesses when seeking financing. We've designed our processes to be more inclusive, responsive, and tailored to meet real-world needs.

RDB Certificate for Company

Marital status certificate

7

Collateral specifications and valuation report

Repayment source verification i.e., issued invoices

Tax clearance certificate

Clean CRB report
